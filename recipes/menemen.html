Menemen

Description

Menemen is a simple yet beloved Turkish breakfast dish made of scrambled eggs, tomatoes, and peppers. It highlights the Turkish emphasis on fresh, flavorful breakfast spreads and communal meals.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green peppers, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Red pepper flakes optional

Steps

  1. Saute onions and peppers in olive oil until softened.
  2. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook for 5-7 minutes until they break down.
  3. Crack the eggs into the pan and gently stir to scramble.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  5. Serve with fresh bread.
