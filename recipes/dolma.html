Dolma

Description

Dolma is a versatile dish that can be made with grape leaves or vegetables like peppers and zucchini. It represents the Ottoman tradition of stuffing vegetables, highlighting the Turkish love for seasonal ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 12 grape leaves (or peppers, zucchinis)
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons pine nuts
  • 2 tablespoons currants
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Saute onions in olive oil, then add rice, pine nuts, currants, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add water and cook the rice until soft.
  3. Stuff grape leaves or vegetables with the rice mixture and roll tightly.
  4. Place them in a pot, cover with water, and simmer for 40 minutes.
  5. Serve cold or at room temperature with lemon.
