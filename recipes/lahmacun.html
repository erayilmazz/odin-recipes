Lahmacun

Description

Known as "Turkish pizza," lahmacun is a thin, crispy flatbread topped with minced meat and spices. Its an affordable and popular street food across Turkey, showing the influence of quick, accessible meals in Turkish culture.

Ingredients

  • 250g ground lamb or beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • 4 flatbreads

Steps

  1. Mix the ground meat with chopped onion, tomatoes, tomato paste, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Spread the mixture thinly over flatbreads.
  3. Bake in a hot oven (220C/430F) for 10-12 minutes until crispy.
  4. Serve with parsley, lemon, and sumac, rolling the flatbread up to eat.
