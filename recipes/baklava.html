Baklava

Description

Baklava is a celebrated dessert in Turkey and other Middle Eastern countries. It is made during special occasions and holidays, symbolizing hospitality and festivity.

Ingredients

  • 500g phyllo dough
  • 250g butter, melted
  • 2 cups walnuts or pistachios, finely chopped
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps

  1. Layer half the phyllo dough sheets in a baking dish, brushing each with melted butter.
  2. Sprinkle chopped nuts and cinnamon over the dough.
  3. Add the remaining phyllo layers, brushing with butter.
  4. Cut into diamond shapes and bake at 180C (350F) for 45 minutes until golden.
  5. Make a syrup by boiling sugar, water, and lemon juice, and pour it over the baklava once its baked.
