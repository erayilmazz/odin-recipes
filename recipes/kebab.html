Kebab

Description

Kebap is a symbol of Turkish cuisine and is deeply rooted in the country's history, with regional variations like Adana, Urfa, and Ä°skender kebabs. It showcases the Turkish mastery of grilling meats.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground lamb or beef (or a mix)
  • 1 onion, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • Skewers

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine ground meat, grated onion, garlic, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix well and shape the meat around skewers.
  3. Grill over medium heat until cooked through, turning frequently.
  4. Serve with flatbread, grilled vegetables, and yogurt sauce.
